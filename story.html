Our Story

From Idea to Implementation


2018 - The Seed

The journey of Maid in India began as a simple Entrepreneurship Management assignment by Kushboo Doshi and Reya Doshi. An idea written on a single sheet of paper gradually evolved into a structured vision.

2025 - The Blueprint

This phase focused on planning and system development. We formulated safety protocols, structured employment models, transparent pricing systems, and ethical workforce standards.

2026 - The Launch

The official launch marked the transformation of purpose into practice. The first prototype deployment demonstrated how organized systems can replace unstructured domestic hiring with professional management.

Our Vision

To create a nationally recognized domestic service platform that ensures dignified employment opportunities and reliable household support.

Our Mission

To formalize the domestic workforce sector by introducing verification systems, training standards, and accountability structures across all service levels.
